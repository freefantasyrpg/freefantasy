Pickpocket

Levels: 1 - 3

Thief Apprentices are beginners who have only recently stepped into the shadowy world of stealth and subtlety. They possess basic skills in lockpicking, sneaking, and simple tricks, but lack the refined technique of seasoned thieves. Apprentices rely on caution, improvisation, and quick thinking rather than mastery. Their talents are still developing, and every successful infiltration or escape teaches them something new. Though inexperienced, they are agile, curious, and eager to prove themselves.

Overview

Requirements

  • Attributes: Dexterity 9, Intelligence 9

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: One-handed melee weapons, sling, short bow, hand crossbow
  • Armor: Light armor (AC 12)

Assassin

Levels: 4 - 10

Assassins are elite operatives trained in silent movement, precise strikes, and the art of eliminating targets without leaving a trace. Their skills extend far beyond common thievery: they study anatomy, poisons, timing, and psychological manipulation. Assassins excel at infiltration, ambushes, and missions requiring absolute discretion. They act with discipline and cold efficiency, turning preparation and patience into deadly tools. An assassin’s presence is rarely noticed — only their results.

Overview

Requirements

  • Attributes: Dexterity 15, Intelligence 12

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: One-handed melee weapons, sling, short bow, hand crossbow
  • Armor: Light armor (AC 12)
  • Level 4: Backstab Sneaking behind an enemy grants a +4 to attack and does level d6 damage. This requires a successful Move silently roll.
  • Level 7: Shadow step Once per encounter, you may reposition up to 5m without being detected. Grants +4 to Hide in Shadows for the next action.
  • Level 10: Death mark For the next attack against that target, you gain +4 to hit and deal × level d6 damage. If the attack kills the target, you remain hidden automatically.

Bard

Levels: 4 - 10

Bards are charismatic performers, storytellers, and wanderers who blend artistry with subtle skill. Their talents allow them to move through social circles with ease, gathering secrets, influencing crowds, and distracting foes when needed. A bard’s voice, music, or tales can inspire allies or unsettle enemies. While not thieves in the traditional sense, their cleverness, adaptability, and social finesse make them invaluable in situations where charm is more effective than stealth. Bards thrive on wit, creativity, and the power of narrative.

Overview

Requirements

  • Attributes: Dexterity 12, Intelligence 12, Charisma 15

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: One-handed melee weapons, sling, short bow, hand crossbow
  • Armor: Light armor (AC 12)
  • Level 4: Alter mood A song that turns enemies into friends. (+2 Reaction)
  • Level 7: Inspire courage Allies within the same zone gain +2 to attack rolls for one round. Can be used once per encounter.
  • Level 10: Song of command One creature must make a saving throw (DC 20). On failure, it obeys a single short command (no self‑harm). On success, it suffers –2 to attack for one round.

Thief

Levels: 4 - 10

Trained Thieves are accomplished specialists who have mastered the core disciplines of their craft. They move confidently through locked doors, guarded halls, and dangerous streets. Their abilities include advanced lockpicking, trap handling, silent movement, and complex sleight‑of‑hand techniques. Unlike apprentices, trained thieves operate with precision and foresight, often planning several steps ahead. They are reliable infiltrators, scouts, and information gatherers — professionals who understand both the risks and the rewards of their trade.

Overview

Requirements

  • Attributes: Dexterity 15, Intelligence 12

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: One-handed melee weapons, sling, short bow, hand crossbow
  • Armor: Light armor (AC 12)
  • Level 4: Überpickpocket +2 to pickpocket, open locks, and disarm traps
  • Level 7: Master of shadows You gain +2 to Move Silently and +2 to Hide in Shadows permanently. Additionally, you may attempt both actions in the same round without penalty.
  • Level 10: Perfct heist Once per day, you may treat any thief skill roll (pickpocket, open locks, disarm traps, move silently, hide in shadows) as if you rolled 30. This automatically grants the special bonus effect.
