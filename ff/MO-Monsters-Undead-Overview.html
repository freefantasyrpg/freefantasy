Undead

Undead are among the worst sights you can run into. They are extremely dangerous and some have very terrifing abilities.

Fear

Everyone who sees an undead must roll a WIS save.

  • Those who fail are frozen in terror and must reroll each round until they succeed.
  • Those who succeed can act normally.

Life drain

Every undead creature has the potential to drain experience points (XP) or even an entire level.

  • About every 6. undead (1 in 6 chance)
    • Drains as much XP as it deals normal damage
  • Every 20. undead (1 in 20 chance)
    • Some more terrible undead creatures can cut the victim’s XP in half, effectively removing a whole level.

Morale

Undead never check morale.
