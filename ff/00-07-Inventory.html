Inventory

Overview

FF uses an abstract inventory system based on slots.
State Slots used Effect Movement*
Unencumbered Up to STR
(e.g. STR 12: 0 - 12 slots)		 No problem. Normal (12m)
Encumbered Exceeding by up to 3 slots
(e.g. STR 12: 13 - 15 slots)		 • -2 to STR/CON checks
• -2 to hide and climb 		Slower (9m)
Encumbered Exceeding by up to 6 slots
(e.g. STR 12: 16 - 18 slots)		 • -5 to all d20 checks.
• -2 to attacks. 		Creeping (6m)
Overloaded More than STR + 6 Slots
(e.g. STR 12: 19 or more slots)		 No d20 checks possible. 0m
