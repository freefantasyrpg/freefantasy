Dwarfes

Overview

Dwarfes are sturdy, resilient folk who excel in craftsmanship, mining, and engineering. They build strongholds deep within mountains or stone‑carved cities renowned for their durability and beauty. Dwarfes value tradition, loyalty, and mastery of their craft. Their physical toughness and disciplined mindset make them reliable allies and formidable opponents, especially in harsh environments.
