Dexterity (DEX)

Overview

Represents agility, reflexes, and coordination. Influences ranged attacks, dodging, stealth, and finesse-based actions.

Missile attacks and damage

The bonus is applied in addition to the attack bonus listed in the class experience table.

Saving throws and usage

The DEX bonus is applied to feats of balance or physical damage that can be dodged.
