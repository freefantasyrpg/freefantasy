Warrior

Overview

Warriors represent the disciplined martial backbone of your world’s adventuring traditions. They rely on physical strength, battlefield awareness, and weapon mastery to overcome threats. While all warriors share a foundation of combat training, their paths diverge as they grow in experience, temperament, and philosophy.

Initiate

Levels: 1 - 3

Overview

Initiates are novice combatants who have begun formal training but have not yet committed to a specific martial discipline. They are adaptable, eager, and still discovering their personal fighting style.

Requirements

  • Attributes: Strenght 9, Constitution 9

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: All
  • Armor: Light armor (AC 12)
  • Weapon of choice: Fighters receive a +2 damage bonus for a paticular weapon

Berserker

Levels: 4 - 10

Overview

A Berserker is a savage, rage-driven warrior who completely abandons caution in favor of overwhelming, ferocious violence.

Requirements

  • Attributes: Strenght 17, Constitution 15, Wisdom 10 (max)

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: All
  • Armor: Medium armor (AC 14)
  • Level 4: Rage Once per day a berserker can start a rage, giving +2 AC, +2 attack, and +2 damage
  • Level 7: Reckless attack Reduces the target’s AC to 10 (only for the berserker); your AC drops by half (minimum 10)
  • Level 10: Execution Once per day you can instantly kill any target that has less than 30% of their hp left.

Hindraces

  • Magical items: A berserker never uses willingly any magical item

Fighter

Levels: 4 - 10

Overview

A Fighter is the quintessential martial expert characterized by supreme discipline, versatility, and mastery of weaponry and armor.

Requirements

  • Attributes: Strenght 17, Constitution 15

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: All
  • Armor: All
  • Level 4: Multiple attacks 2 attacks per round.
  • Level 7: Sacrifice shield Avoid all physical damage in a round
  • Level 10: Counter-Thrust When an enemy misses you, instantly deliver a guaranteed critical strike.

Ranger

Levels: 4 - 10

Overview

A Ranger’s animal companion is more than a trained pet; it is a spiritual equal and an extension of the Ranger’s own soul. They share an unspoken, supernatural bond. Because their connection is deeply empathetic rather than rigidly commanding, the Ranger perceives the beast’s emotions, allowing them to instantly sense fear, joy, or distress in real-time.

Requirements

  • Attributes: Strenght 13, Dexterity 13, Constitution 15, Wisdom 15

Skills and powers

  • Weapons: All
  • Armor: Light armor (AC 12)
  • Level 4: Companion Rangers choose an animal companion.
  • Level 7: Ghost Step Become completely invisible and untargetable for two rounds. Only usable in natural surroundings.
  • Level 10: Hunter’s Mark Mark a single target; your attacks bypass all of their armor.
