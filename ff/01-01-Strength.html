Strength (STR)

Overview

Measures physical power. Governs melee damage, lifting, pushing, breaking, and general brawn.

Melee attacks and damage

The bonus is applied in addition to the attack bonus listed in the class experience table.

The bonus also applies to the damage of all melee and thrown weapons.

Carrying

You can carry STR or 10 items, whatever is higher.

Saving throws and usage

The STR bonus is applied to feats of strength like breaking a door, lifting heavy objects, or physical damage.
