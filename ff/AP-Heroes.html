Heroes of Free Fantasy

The Warrior

A towering human clad in ringmail, his halberd gleaming in the light of battle. Muscles like forged steel and a gaze that never wavers — he is the embodiment of discipline and raw strength. Few stand their ground when he advances.

Role: Frontline fighter, protector, shock trooper.
Typical Attributes: High Strength, solid Constitution, moderate Dexterity.
Signature Abilities: Powerful melee attacks, battlefield control, defensive stances.
Background Hooks: Veteran of a lost war, former city guard, mercenary seeking redemption.

The Elven Archer

Graceful and silent, her leather armor moves like a second skin. The bow in her hands is an extension of her will — swift, precise, and deadly. Her eyes miss nothing, and her arrows strike before her enemies even notice her presence.

Role: Ranged damage dealer, scout, skirmisher.
Typical Attributes: High Dexterity, keen Wisdom, light but useful Strength.
Signature Abilities: Precise shots, called targets, rapid volleys, keen senses.
Background Hooks: Hunter of corrupted beasts, exile from an elven court, wanderer bound to an ancient oath.

The Dwarven Warrior

Short, broad, and unyielding. His pickaxe is both weapon and symbol — forged for war and labor alike. Beneath his helmet, his beard is braided with iron rings, each marking a victory earned through stubborn courage.

Role: Durable melee fighter, tunnel guardian, siege breaker.
Typical Attributes: High Constitution, strong Strength, resolute Wisdom.
Signature Abilities: Resilience, armor mastery, brutal close‑quarters strikes, expertise in stone and metal.
Background Hooks: Defender of a besieged hold, miner turned warrior, oathbound to reclaim lost halls.

The Wizard

A figure wrapped in shadow, his robe concealing all but the faint outline of his chin. He speaks little, yet the air around him hums with restrained power. His presence alone bends silence into reverence — or fear.

Role: Arcane caster, problem solver, wielder of dangerous knowledge.
Typical Attributes: High Intelligence, focused Will, fragile but sharp Constitution.
Signature Abilities: Versatile spells, control effects, rituals, lore of forgotten places.
Background Hooks: Student of a forbidden grimoire, survivor of a failed cabal, wanderer seeking a single lost spell.

The Druid

Clad in green robes adorned with fur and leaves, he carries a staff crowned with a glowing crystal. His antlered headdress marks him as one with nature’s will. Calm and ancient, his eyes reflect the wisdom of forests older than kingdoms.

Role: Nature caster, healer, guide between wilds and civilization.
Typical Attributes: High Wisdom, steady Constitution, grounded Charisma.
Signature Abilities: Nature magic, healing, animal bonds, control over terrain and weather.
Background Hooks: Guardian of a sacred grove, emissary of ancient spirits, wanderer sent to correct a growing imbalance.
