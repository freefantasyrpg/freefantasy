Attributes

Overview

The six attributes (STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA) are the core stats that define a character’s natural strengths and weaknesses.

Rolling or choosing attributes

Roll 6 times 3d6 or distribute the following numbers: 15, 12, 10, 9, 9, 7 amount the attributes. Don't forget to write down the bonus of every attribute.

Improving attributes

Everytime you reach a new level for the first time you can increase one attribute of your choice.
