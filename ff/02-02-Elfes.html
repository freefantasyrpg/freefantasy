Elfes and Half-elfes

Overview

Elfes are long‑lived beings with a deep connection to nature, magic, or ancient traditions, depending on their culture. They are known for keen senses, grace, and a calm, thoughtful demeanor. Elfes often value harmony, craftsmanship, and knowledge, preserving lore that spans centuries. Their extended lifespan gives them a broader perspective on events, though it can also make them cautious or slow to act.
