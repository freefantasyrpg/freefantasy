Halflings

Overview

Halflings are small, nimble people known for their resourcefulness and warm, community-focused lifestyles. They prefer peaceful settlements, fertile lands, and simple comforts, yet they are surprisingly brave when circumstances demand it. Halflings rely on agility, cleverness, and a keen sense of awareness rather than brute strength. Their optimism and adaptability make them welcome companions in any adventuring group.
