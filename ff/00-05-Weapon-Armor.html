Weapons and armor

Overview

Weapons and armor are simplified compared to other games. The focus is on roleplay, not on strict accuracy.
Weapons can be anything you imagine — remember, the class defines the damage.
A plate mail can be completely useless (AC 10) and cost nothing, or fortified (AC 16) and cost 800 gp. Any armor purchased as light armor grants AC 12, and any armor purchased as medium armor grants AC 14.
