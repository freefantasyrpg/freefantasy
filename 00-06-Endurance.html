Endurance

Overview

Endurance replaces HP. Its regenerated very quickly.

Endurance

After Endurance is reduced to 0, damage is applied to attributes in the following order: Constitution (CON), Strength (STR), Dexterity (DEX).

Regeneration

Endurance is fully restored after a short rest. This can be done twice a day.

Attribute damage requires magical healing or medical treatment.
